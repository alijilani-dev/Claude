### TensorFlow

**Gap Type**: missing  
**Priority**: HIGH

#### Questions:

1. Explain the core concepts and principles of TensorFlow.

2. What are the main use cases and applications of TensorFlow?

3. Describe the key features that make TensorFlow useful.

4. What are common alternatives to TensorFlow and how do they compare?

5. Describe a time you learned a new technology similar to TensorFlow. What was your approach?

6. Describe a challenging problem you would solve using TensorFlow.

7. How would you debug an issue with TensorFlow in a production environment?

8. Design a system architecture that leverages TensorFlow effectively.

### PyTorch

**Gap Type**: missing  
**Priority**: HIGH

#### Questions:

1. Explain the difference between PyTorch and TensorFlow in terms of computational graphs.

2. What is a PyTorch tensor and how does it differ from a NumPy array?

3. Describe the concept of autograd in PyTorch and how automatic differentiation works.

4. How do you define a custom neural network layer in PyTorch?

5. Describe a time you learned a new technology similar to PyTorch. What was your approach?

6. Your PyTorch model is overfitting on the training data. What techniques would you use to address this?

7. You need to train a model on multiple GPUs. How would you implement distributed training in PyTorch?

8. A PyTorch model is running slowly during inference. How would you optimize it for production?

### Kubernetes

**Gap Type**: weak  
**Priority**: MEDIUM

#### Questions:

1. Explain advanced features of Kubernetes that optimize performance.

2. What are common pitfalls and anti-patterns when working with Kubernetes at scale?

3. Compare Kubernetes with alternative technologies. When would you choose Kubernetes?

4. Describe a complex problem you solved using Kubernetes. Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

5. A production pod keeps crashing and restarting. Walk me through your debugging process using kubectl.

6. You need to deploy a stateful application (like a database) on Kubernetes. How would you approach this?

7. Your team wants to implement blue-green deployments. How would you configure this in Kubernetes?

8. A service is experiencing high latency. How would you investigate and resolve this in a Kubernetes environment?

9. How would you implement zero-downtime deployments in Kubernetes?

10. Design a Kubernetes deployment strategy for a microservices application with 10 services.

## Study Recommendations

### For Missing Skills (High Priority)
1. Take an online course or tutorial
2. Build a small project to gain hands-on experience
3. Read official documentation
4. Practice with sample problems or exercises

### For Weak Skills (Medium Priority)
1. Deepen your understanding with advanced tutorials
2. Study best practices and design patterns
3. Review production-scale use cases
4. Practice explaining concepts clearly

### Interview Preparation Tips
- Practice answering questions out loud
- Use the STAR method for behavioral questions
- Prepare specific examples from your experience
- Research the company and role thoroughly
- Have questions ready to ask the interviewer
