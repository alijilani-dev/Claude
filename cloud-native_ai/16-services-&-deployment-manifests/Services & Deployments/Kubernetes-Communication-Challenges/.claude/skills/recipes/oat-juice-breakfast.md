# Oat Juice Breakfast Bowl

## Description
A refreshing, nutritious breakfast featuring homemade oat juice with colorful toppings. This recipe is completely nut-free, allergy-safe, and packed with wholesome ingredients.

## Servings
2 servings

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Ingredients

### For the Oat Juice:
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 4 cups cold water
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### For the Breakfast Bowl:
- 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
- 1/4 cup granola (nut-free)
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons shredded coconut
- Fresh mint leaves for garnish

## Instructions

### Making the Oat Juice:
1. Add rolled oats and cold water to a blender
2. Blend on high speed for 30-45 seconds until smooth
3. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a pitcher
4. Stir in maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt
5. Refrigerate until ready to serve

### Assembling the Breakfast Bowl:
1. Pour 1 cup of oat juice into each bowl
2. Arrange strawberries on one side (bright red)
3. Place banana slices in the center (bright yellow)
4. Add blueberries on the other side (vibrant blue/purple)
5. Sprinkle chia seeds and granola on top
6. Add shredded coconut for texture
7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a pop of green

## Plating Style
Use **bright, simple colors** for visual appeal:
- **Red** from strawberries
- **Yellow** from bananas
- **Blue/Purple** from blueberries
- **Green** from mint
- **White** from coconut and oat juice

Arrange fruits in separate sections for a colorful, eye-catching presentation.

## Nutritional Benefits
- High in fiber from oats and chia seeds
- Rich in antioxidants from berries
- Natural energy from bananas
- Plant-based and dairy-free
- Heart-healthy and cholesterol-free

## Allergy Information
✓ **Nut-free** - No peanuts or tree nuts
✓ **Dairy-free**
✓ **Vegan-friendly**
✓ Safe for common allergies

## Storage
Oat juice can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Shake well before serving.
